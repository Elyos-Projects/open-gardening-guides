**open-gardening-guides** turns spare AI capacity into a free, open, **climate-zone-aware** body of food-growing guidance for households and communities working to feed themselves. Its purpose is narrow and honest: tell a person *what food crops they can realistically grow where they actually live, and what to do in each month of their season* — sourced from authoritative agronomy, traceable to a named source, and usable offline and on paper.

**Definition of shipped:** Output adopted/used by a real beneficiary partner.
